Favorite Stop Motion Movies Seen This Year

A List By Anjanaa

1. The Little Prince

This movie is adapted from a french childerns' story book and deals with topics like growing up, relationships and parting away. I enjoyed watching this movie.

2. Coraline

This movie is also adapted from a childrens' story book of the same name. This movie is very spooky and deals with interdimentional horror, loneliness and family. I really enjoyed the nuances and animation.

3. The Memoir Of The Snail

This is the only movie in this list that is not for children and was released this year. The movie takes through the story or 'Memoir' of the protagonist 'Grace' and her twin 'Gilbert'.
